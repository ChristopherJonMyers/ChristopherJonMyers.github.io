What Poetry Is To Me

 My first quality experience with poetry came when I enrolled in a Creative Writing course at my university. Throughout this class the professor provided a space for creative expression through different forms of creative writing. Poetry is where I found my niche.
 Poetry gave me a tool I had not been able to find before. What I mean is, poetry allowed me to release my compartmentalized angst in a healthy, almost therapeutic way. This time in my life was one filled with many existential questions that I needed to find answers for. Poetry became a tangible way for me to explore those question while keeping myself tethered to reality.
