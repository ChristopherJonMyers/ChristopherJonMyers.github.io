Myself Separated into Stanzas

 This portfolio was created as a project for my Creative Writing course. The assignment was to compose ten poems in which show my understanding of various poetry skills. I would like to explain that this is a starting point for me. Looking back and reading what I created makes me want to print out the portfolio and burn it. However, I understand the importance of seeing oneâ€™s progress and seeing growth. Therefore, I a choosing to immortalize my mediocre poetry on the internet. Hopefully, these poems will express that poetry, like every creative field, is an acquired skill that must be learned.

 The title of the portfolio serves as thumbnail of what my mindset was for most of the writing process. On reread, it is clear to see my desperate and failed attempts to force meaning and say something of importance in my poetry. This mindset probably came about by listing to performance poetry and reading heady, philosophical poetry. Regardless, enjoy the cringe.
